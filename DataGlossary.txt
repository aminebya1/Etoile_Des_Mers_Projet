passenger_id: Unique identifier for the passenger.
survived: Indicates whether the passenger survived (1) or not (0).
class: Ticket class of the passenger (1, 2, or 3).
name: Name of the passenger.
sex: Gender of the passenger (male or female).
age: Age of the passenger.
sibsp: Number of siblings or spouses aboard.
parch: Number of parents or children aboard.
ticket: Ticket number of the passenger.
fare: Fare paid by the passenger.
cabin: Cabin number of the passenger.
embarked: Port of embarkation (C = Cherbourg, Q = Queenstown, S = Southampton).
date_embarked: Date of passenger's embarkation.
workclass: Category of the passenger's working class.
education: Education level of the passenger.
marital_status: Marital status of the passenger.
occupation: Occupation of the passenger.
relationship: Work roles.
capital_gain: Capital gain of the passenger.
capital_loss: Capital loss of the passenger.
hours_per_week: Number of hours worked per week.
native_country: Native country of the passenger.
income: Income of the passenger (categorized as >50K or <=50K).
